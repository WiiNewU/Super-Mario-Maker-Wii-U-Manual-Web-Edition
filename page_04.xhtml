Coursebot

Coursebot is here to help you manage the courses you've saved. Courses created by other makers appear with , and courses you've uploaded are marked with . Coursebot is a clever fellow, so he can remember up to 120 courses at once!

  • Edit
    Load a saved course to make changes.
    Play
    Play through a course.
  • Upload
    Upload a course to share it with other players.
    Change Name
    Change the name of a course. This will allow you to upload it as a new course.
  • Delete
    Delete a course from Coursebot's memory.

    ◆ To delete a course from the server after you've uploaded it, select Uploaded Courses from your own User Info page.

Once you save over a course or delete it, the original data is lost forever. When saving changes or deleting courses, you should be very careful to avoid losing data!

Switching Panels

Touch the button in the top left to switch between Coursebot's panels.

  • My Courses
    This panel shows courses you have created.
    Sample Courses
    This panel includes sample courses, such as those you have cleared in 10 Mario Challenge.

    ◆ You can't upload courses created by editing sample courses.

Adjusting Courses

Once you've saved a few courses, get experimenting!

  • Making Worlds
    If you save four courses on one row, they'll form a world. You can play them as a set by touching .
    Moving Courses
    If you touch and hold a course, you can drag it to a different slot. This will change the order of the stages when played as a single world.

Settings

Change various settings.

  • Controls
    Change the buttons used to make Mario dash and jump.
    Important Information about Uploading Courses
    Shows important information that you should read before uploading courses.
  • GamePad 3D Audio
    Configures 3D audio. With the Wii U GamePad controller's speakers you can enjoy a 3D-like audio experience, so be sure to crank up the volume on the GamePad when this option is activated!
    Course Comments
    Lets you choose whether to show comments from players all around the world while playing courses or only comments from players in your own console region.
  • Notifications to
    Course Creators
    When you play a course created by another maker, the course creator may be notified every time you are playing the course. You can turn off this notification in Settings. Otherwise, when you play courses created by other makers, the course creators will be notified of the following actions:
    • ● When you play a course
    • ● When you star a course
    • ● When you comment on a course

    ◆ Even if you opt out of notifications, your play data will still be saved to the course's history.

    Notifications about
    My Courses
    A notification will be displayed every time another user is playing a course you created. You can turn off this notification in Settings. Otherwise, when other users play courses you created, pop-ups will appear to let you know when the following things happen:
    • ● When someone plays one of your courses
    • ● When someone stars one of your courses
    • ● When someone leaves a comment for you

    ◆ Even if you choose not to display notifications in real time, you can always check them later from the main menu.
