Controlling Mario

From 8-bit to the modern era!

Mario's controls differ slightly depending on the current game style. A truly skillful maker knows that the button layout can be adjusted by selecting the Settings icon from the main menu.

But I'm sure you're almost as skilled as my feathered friend! I bet you'll have no problem mastering the controls for every type of Mario.

◆ The Wii U Pro Controller and Classic Controller Pro use the same controls as the Wii U GamePad.

Controlling Mario's Transformations

Costume Mario

The Mystery Mushroom will turn Mario into Costume Mario, dressing him up as various characters or items. When wearing a costume, Mario is controlled in the same way as a non-powered-up Mario.
